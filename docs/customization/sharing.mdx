# Sharing Your Profile
After customizing your profile to your liking, you can share it with your unique **emogir.ls** with your audience on social media, websites and more.

## How can I share my profile?
There are many ways to share your **emogir.ls** profile, here are some of them: 
- On social media platforms like [**Tiktok**](https://tiktok.com) [**Instagram**](https://instagram.com) or any other platform.
- On your [**Discord**](https://discord.com) biography or status.
- On your personal website 

## How can I generate a emogir.ls QR code?
To get a QR code for your profile, follow these steps:
- Go to [emogir.ls/dashboard](https://emogir.ls/dashboard)
- Find the "Share Your Profile" button, and click on it
- Click on "emogir.ls QR Code" or "Copy URL" and share it with your preferred method
