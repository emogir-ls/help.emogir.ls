# Exploring your Options

**emogir.ls** gives you countless options in order to make your bio-link feel truely your own. To get started with customizing your page, navigate to `emogir.ls/dashboard/customize`

<Button href="https://emogir.ls/dashboard/customize">Customize your page</Button>

## Sections:

- **Assets** - manage your custom assets
- **Profile** - specify displayed text
- **Layout** - configure how elements are displayed
- **Typography** - configure fonts + enter text
- **Display Options** - small additional elements
- **About Me Styling** - configure the about me section
- **Colors** - color configuration
- **Animations** - animations for certain elements
- **Icons** - configure all aspects of page Icons
- **Volume Control** - toggle/configure volume control
- **Borders & Effects** - toggle/configure borders and effects
- **Typewriter** - configure the typewriter effects
- **Music Player** - toggle/configure the music players
- **Widgets** - configure widgets
- **Comments** - toggle/configure comments
- **Likes** - toggle/configure likes
