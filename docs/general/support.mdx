# Getting Account Support

Our **emogir.ls** support team is always here to help. For faster answers, we recommend checking the documentation or chatting with the emogir.ls Chat Bot in the bottom-right corner.

## Changing Your Username, Alias, or Display Name
To change your **username**, or **alias**, follow these **steps:**
- Go to [emogir.ls/dashboard/settings](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/settings)
- From there you can change your username, and manage your aliases

<Img src="/img/general/support/username-and-alias.png" alt="Username and Aliases in Settings Page." />

<Admonition type="info" title="Information">
  Username changes have a **5-day cooldown**, while alias edits are subject to a **6-hour cooldown**.
</Admonition>

To change your **display name**, follow these **steps:**
- Go to [emogir.ls/dashboard/customize](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/customize)
- Find the **Profile** section and edit your Display Name.

<Img src="/img/general/support/display-name.png" alt="Display Name setting in Customization Page." />

You can find many more settings in the [emogir.ls/dashboard/settings](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/settings) page such as **changing your email address** and **changing your password**.

## Logging in with Discord
By default, all accounts that have a **discord account linked** would have the option to [Login with Discord](https://emogir.ls/auth/login). 

<Button href="/">Connecting your Discord Account</Button>

<Admonition type="info" title="Information">
  If your Discord account was linked before **December, 31st 2025** you must re-link it to enable **Login with Discord**.
</Admonition>
