## Profile Analytics {#profile-analytics}

Profile analytics give you a clear view of how your profile is performing, including the number of views and how visitors discovered your profile. These insights help you measure your reach and engagement, so you can make informed decisions about your content and presence.

## General Analytics {#general-analytics}

<Img src="/img/general/analytics/general-analytics.png" alt="General Analayitcs card in Dashboard." />

The cards above provide an **overview** of the following:
- **Total Link Clicks:** Displays the total number of clicks your links have received over the selected time period.  
- **Click Rate:** Shows the percentage of visitors who clicked on your links compared to total profile views.  
- **Profile Views:** The total number of times your profile has been viewed.  
- **Average Daily Views:** Shows the average number of profile views per day.  
- **Profile Views Graph:** Visualizes how your profile views have changed over time.  
- **Visitor Devices:** Displays the distribution of devices (Desktop vs Mobile) used to view your profile.  

## Advanced Analytics {#advanced-analytics}

<Img src="/img/general/analytics/advanced-analytics.png" alt="Advanced Analytics card in Dashboard." />

The cards above provide an **overview** of the following:

- **Traffic & Engagement:** Track your link clicks, traffic sources, and social media breakdown to see how users interact with your profile.  
- **Geographic Data:** See where your visitors are coming from around the world.  
- **Top Countries & Regional Performance:** Identify which countries and regions are driving the most traffic.  
- **Audience Insights & Growth Trends:** Understand your audience, including new vs. returning visitors, and monitor growth trends week-over-week and month-over-month.  
- **Hourly Patterns:** Discover peak times when your profile receives the most views.  
- **And More:** Additional metrics and insights to help you optimize your profile and engagement.
