# Trouble Shooting & Issues

If you’re experiencing general issues with [emogir.ls](https://emogir.ls), you can find solutions to common problems here. If you can’t find what you’re looking for, try using the chat bot or contact us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/emogirls)

## Common Issues {#common-issues}

### Not Receiving my Verification or Password Reset Emails {#not-receiving-my-verification-or-password-reset-emails}
- Check your spam folder
- Double check the email you have entered

<Admonition type="tip" title="Tip">
  If you haven’t received your **emogir.ls** verification code, go to the login page and enter your details to request a new email.  
  If your account is still unverified, a verification link will also be automatically sent within 6 hours of account creation.
</Admonition>

### My assets are not uploading {#my-assets-are-not-uploading}
- Ensure you are uploading the correct asset type 
- Verify that your asset does not exceed the size limit  
- Check that your internet connection is stable  

### I can't log in to my account {#i-cant-log-in-to-my-account}
- Ensure you have entered the correct email address and password  
- If the details are correct but you’re still seeing an invalid credentials error, use the **Forgot Password** option on the login page  

### I can't log in with Discord {#i-cant-log-in-with-discord}
- If your Discord account was connected before **December 31st, 2025**, please reconnect it on the [Dashboard Settings](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/settings) page  
- Ensure your Discord account is not linked to multiple **emogir.ls** accounts  

## Can't find your issue? {#cant-find-your-issue}
If you are still experiencing problems and cannot find a solution in the documentation or the chat bot, please contact us for assistance:  
- Email: [support@emogir.ls](mailto:help@emogir.ls)  
- Discord: [discord.gg/emogirls](https://discord.gg/emogirls)
