# Contact Us

You can connect with other users and get instant help by joining our [Discord community](https://discord.gg/emogirls) where we also share news and updates.


## Report a Bug {#report-a-bug}
If you notice a bug or experience a technical issue on **emogir.ls**, let us know so we can fix it quickly.  
You can report issues via:  
- **Email:** [support@emogir.ls](mailto:support@emogir.ls)  
- **Discord:** Share the problem with our [community](https://discord.gg/emogirls) for support and updates
