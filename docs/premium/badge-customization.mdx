# Badge Customization & Controls
<h4>
Badges allow you to showcase your achievements on emogir.ls
</h4>
With emogir.ls Premium, you can reorder, recolor, & toggle each badge on and off individually

---

## Badges Tab

On the [badges tab](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/badges) you can see information about any badge

<Admonition type="info" title="Information">
You can only edit, equip, and modify badges that you **own**.
</Admonition>
---

## Reordering badges

Badges that are at the top of your list will show first.

1. Head to **[Account → Badges](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/badges)**.  
2. Find the badge you want to reorder.  
3. Hold the **icon** on the left of the badge.  
4. Drag the badge to your desired position.  
5. Release to save your changes.

---

## Toggling badges on/off

Sometimes you might want to hide a badge so it doesn't show on your profile.

To do that,

1. Head to **[Account → Badges](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/badges)**.  
2. Locate the badge you want to toggle.
3. Click on the badge to toggle it.

<Admonition type="info" title="Information">
You can always click on the badge again to toggle its visiblity again.
</Admonition>
