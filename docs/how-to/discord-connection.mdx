# Connecting your Discord Account

You can connect your Discord account to **emogir.ls** to enable **Discord Presence** on your profile. This feature allows you to display your current Discord status directly on your emogir.ls profile, giving visitors a real-time view of your Discord activity.

## Benefits

- **Show your Discord status** on your profile
- **Display your current activity** to visitors
- **Enhance your profile** with live Discord presence

## Linking your Discord Account
To link your Discord account to **emogir.ls**, follow these steps:
- Head over to [emogir.ls/dashboard/settings](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/settings)
- Scroll Down, then click on "Connect Discord" 
- After completion of **connection** you will be redirected back to emogir.ls!

<Img src="/img/how-to/discord/connecting-discord.png" alt="Connect Discord Option." />

<Admonition type="info">
Your Discord status won't appear on your profile until you add the Discord widget. Once added, your status will automatically update whenever it changes.
</Admonition>

## Enable Discord Presence 
After linking your Discord account to **emogir.ls** you can enable the emogir.ls Presence widget by following these steps:
- Head over to [emogir.ls/dashboard/widgets](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/widgets)
- Click on the "**Discord**" widget, select the type as **Presence** then click **Add**

<Img src="/img/how-to/discord/saving-presence.png" alt="Displaying Presence as a Widget." />

## Claiming your Roles on emogir.ls Discord Server 
Certain badges that you can claim and receive on emogir.ls are linked to roles on the official [emogirls server](https://discord.gg/emogirls)
To claim these badges, follow these steps:
- Go to [emogir.ls/dashboard/settings](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/settings)
- Find the "Claim Premium Discord Role" button & click on it

<Img src="/img/how-to/discord/claiming-role.png" alt="Claiming Discord Roles." />

## Unlining your Discord Account
If you want to unlink your Discord Connection from emogir.ls, follow these steps:
- Go to [emogir.ls/dashboard/settings](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/settings)
- Scroll down to your Discord Connection 
- Click on **Disconnect**
