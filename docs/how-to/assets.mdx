# Uploading Assets

Head over to [https://emogir.ls/dashboard/customize](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/customize)

- Click the asset you want to upload (avatar, banner, music, custom cursor) and select your file
- Select your file
- **Save your changes**

### File limits
| Type             | Allowed File Types | Max File Size |
|:----------------:|:------------------:|:-------------:|
| Avatar           | `PNG`, `JPG`, `JPEG`, `GIF`, `WebP` | 10 MB |
| Custom Cursor    | `CUR`, `PNG` | 5 MB |
| Music Player     | `MP3`, `WAV`, `OGG`, `M4A` | 20 MB |
| Audio Cover      | `PNG`, `JPG`, `JPEG`, `WebP` | 5 MB |
| Background Image | `PNG`, `JPG`, `JPEG`, `GIF`, `WebP` | 50 MB |
| Custom Font      | `TTF`, `OTF`, `WOFF`, `WOFF2` | 10 MB |
| Link Icon        | `PNG`, `SVG`, `ICO` | 2 MB |

## Player Direction
<Admonition type="info">You can find this on the right of the music player</Admonition>
- The Player Direction is where your music player is on your profile
- Simply select one, and your music player will appear there on your profile
- You can also customize it in the music player card near the bottom

## Profile customization
<Admonition type="info">You can customize the shape of your profile, use a Discord decoration on your profile, and use your Discord avatar</Admonition>

- Use your Discord avatar by clicking "Use Discord Avatar"
- Select a Discord decoration by clicking "Edit Decoration"
- Select a profile shape by clicking on the one you want

**Then, make sure you save your changes!**
