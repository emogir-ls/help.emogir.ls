# Uploading Assets

**Head over to [https://emogir.ls/dashboard/customize](https://emogir.ls/dashboard/customize)**

- **Click the asset you want to upload (avatar, banner, music, custom cursor) and select your file.**
- **Select your file**
- **Save your changes**

## Player Direction
<Admonition type="info">**You can find this on the right of the music player**</Admonition>
- **The Player Direction is where your music player is on your profile**
**Simply select one, and your music player will appear there on your profile.**<br>
<Small>You can also customize it in the music player card near the bottom.<Small>

## Profile customization
<Admonition type="info">**You can find this below your profile**</Admonition>

**You can customize the shape of your profile, use a Discord decoration on your profile, and use your Discord avatar**

- **Use your Discord avatar by clicking "Use Discord Avatar"**<br>
- **Select a Discord decoration by clicking "Edit Decoration"**<br>
- **Select a profile shape by clicking on the same you want**

**Then, make sure you save your changes!**
